Katsudon Recipe

Prepare this easy Katsudon from the anime Yuri on Ice! You can have soft and sticky Japanese rice topped with pork cutlet nestled in pillowy eggs and onions simmered in a sweet-savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tonkatsu/li>
  • Onion
  • Broth - dashi, sugar, mirin and soy sauce
  • Eggs
  • Green onion
  • Short-grain rice

Method

  1. Make Tonkatsu

  2. Cook the onion in the savoury broth til tender

  3. Place Tonkatsu on top to let it absorb the flavours of the broth.

  4. Add the beaten egg mixture and cook til just set

  5. Serve over steamed rice and enjoy!

Source: Just One Cookbook
